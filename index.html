About

My Approach

I'm a software engineer who likes to keep things practical. I've managed projects end-to-endfrom architecture to deploymentand I know how to deliver on time without needing someone to look over my shoulder. My main tools are Go and React, but I'm comfortable with whatever gets the job done. I'm experienced with advanced software designDDD, Clean Architecture, microservicesbut I only use that kind of complexity when it actually solves a real problem.

My focus is always on building things that matter to the business, not just writing code for its own sake. Over the past few years, I've delivered for international clients:

  • Automation tools and bots that save time and cut out manual work
  • Web apps with fast, reliable backends and clean, modern frontends
  • AI-powered reform analysis tool that turns complex documents into structured insights

I work well on my own or as part of a remote team, and I care about shipping results that actually make a difference.
